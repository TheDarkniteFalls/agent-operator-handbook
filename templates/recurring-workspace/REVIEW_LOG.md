# Review Log

Use this log to connect real outcomes and corrections to later improvements.

Record material outputs, not every internal step or routine receipt. Use a
stable output ID so feedback closes the matching item instead of creating
another queue.

| Date | Output ID | Output | Outcome | What Changed | Lesson | Check Next Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Date] | [Stable ID] | [Path or title] | used / edited / rejected / superseded / archived | [Short difference] | [Accepted lesson or none] | [Observable test] |

## Review Rules

- `what_happened` and `recommendation_quality` are separate judgements.
- `wrong_surface` is different from `wrong_content`.
- Re-registering the same output should update its record unless its purpose or
  artifact identity genuinely changed.
- A used, rejected, superseded, or archived item should not remain pending.
- Do not make a durable rule from one ambiguous correction.
